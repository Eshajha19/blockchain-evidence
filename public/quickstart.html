Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes

Prerequisites

  • MetaMask wallet extension installed
  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
  • Internet connection
  • Valid email address

Step 1: Download MetaMask

  1. Visit metamask.io
  2. Click Download
  3. Select your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
  4. Install the extension
  5. Restart your browser

Step 2: Create Your MetaMask Wallet

  1. Click MetaMask icon in browser toolbar
  2. Click "Create a Wallet"
  3. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
  4. IMPORTANT: Save your recovery phrase in a safe place (write it down!)
  5. Confirm your recovery phrase
  6. ‚úÖ Your wallet is created!

Step 3: Connect to EVID-DGC Website

  1. Go to blockchain-evidence.onrender.com
  2. Click "Connect MetaMask" button
  3. Approve the connection in MetaMask popup
  4. ‚úÖ Your wallet is now connected!

Step 4: Select Your Role

Choose from 8 roles:

  1. Public Viewer - View public cases
  2. Investigator - Create cases, upload evidence
  3. Forensic Analyst - Analyze evidence
  4. Legal Professional - Legal review
  5. Court Official - Judicial control
  6. Evidence Manager - Manage storage
  7. Auditor - Audit access
  8. Administrator - Full control

Step 5: Complete Registration

  1. Fill in your profile information:
    • Full Name
    • Badge Number (if applicable)
    • Department
    • Jurisdiction
  2. Click Register

Step 6: Start Using EVID-DGC

  1. Go to your dashboard
  2. Click "Add Evidence" to upload files
  3. Create a new case
  4. Start managing evidence securely on blockchain!
